Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

  • Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website.

How do we use cookies?

  • We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences.

  • Analyze website traffic.

  • Personalize content and advertisements.

  • Improve website functionality.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.

  • Performance cookies: Collect information on how you use our site.

  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

  • Marketing cookies: Used for personalized advertising.

Managing cookies:

  • You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings.

  • Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some website features.
