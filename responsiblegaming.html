Responsible Gaming

At Betcha, we promote a responsible approach to gambling. Learn how to play safely.

1. Our Commitment

  • Our goal is to provide a safe and responsible gaming environment.

  • We promote an informed approach to gambling and prevent gambling addiction.

2. How to Play Responsibly?

  • Treat gambling as entertainment, not a way to make money.

  • Set a budget and never exceed your financial limits.

  • Do not play under the influence of alcohol or emotions.

  • Take regular breaks from playing.

3. Protection of Minors

  • Only individuals over the age of 18 are allowed to access our site.

  • We recommend using parental control software to protect minors.

4. Recognizing a Gambling Problem

  • Do you play longer than you planned?

  • Are you spending more money than you can afford?

  • Is gambling negatively affecting your personal or professional life?

  • If you answered “yes” to any of these questions, consider seeking professional help.

5. Where to Get Help?

  • If you have a gambling problem, contact support organizations:

  • Gambling Helpline New Zealand

  • BeGambleAware

6. Contact

  • Have questions? Contact us at: info@betchaplay.co.nz
